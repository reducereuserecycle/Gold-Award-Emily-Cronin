Frequently Asked Questions



1. Where can I find detailed information about local recycling?

Visit your local recycling company's website! The company name will be on any trash or recycling bins your house uses.





2. Why exactly should I care about composting?

It is an effective way to reduce the amount of waste being put in landfills. Worms decompose the food scraps,
newspaper strips, egg carton bits, etc. and turn them into dirt. Waste levels are decreased and reusable dirt is produced!





3. How can I convince my family to start a compost?

Show them related facts and the composting video on this website! Explain to them that composting is a simple,
home friendly way to reduce waste and create some dirt that can be used to grow flowers or even food.





4. How can I convince my family to create a recycling bin?

Show them related facts and the worm bin video on this website! Explain to them that recycling household items is easy.





5. How can I remember everything that's compostable or recyclable?

Feel free to print out the list of items found on this website under the Fun Facts tab! You can put this list on your
recycling bin or near a compost collection bin to keep your memory refreshed.
